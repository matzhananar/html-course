ABOUT US

 

Our flower company Blossom Almaty is glad to present you a variety of bouquets to please
yourself, loved ones, relatives and friends.
Blossom Almaty believe that everyone should have a little luxury in their life. We apply the
same level of care and attention in our customer service as we do in selecting and
arranging our luxury flowers. We want to make sure your recipient is delighted with
their gift and you are a 100% happy. As we continue to grow, we want to make sure all of
our products are as sustainable as possible. We buy our flowers from British & Kenyan
farms and put back into the community. Only the very best stems are carefully selected,
arranged and packaged by our highly experiezced florists who lovingly hand-tie each
bouquet with a black satin ribbon, ready to surprise and delight our customers.

If you feel something isn't right, our friendly Customer Experience team are available
to chat via email, or on the phone, and love to go the extra mile to ensure you're happy.

Respectfully, the owner of the store Zhibek K
